乌克兰贪腐研究 Corruption in Post-Soviet Ukraine

这是生平听的第一场政治科学报告，马上记录一下。

标题：Corruption in Post-Soviet Ukraine

讲者：Аndriy Meleshevych （乌克兰学者）
网站： https://events.stanford.edu/events/749/74953/
地点：斯坦福大学
日期：2018年2月15日


这个报告令我知道贪腐也是一个政治科学研究课题，贪腐的根源，制定怎样的政策去治理，也是需要研究的。

一开始先介绍了很早期的研究。

- Nathaniel Leff, "Economic Development through Bureaucratic Corruption/' American Behavioral Scientist,1964. （看标题关于权贵贪腐和经济发展，跟中国情况很类似）

- Samuel Huntington, Political Order in Changing Societies,1968 （这位是已故哈佛大学政治科学大师

- Joseph Nye, "Corruption and Political Development: a cost benefit analysis," APSR, 1967

- James Scott, "Corruption, Machine Politics, and Political Change," APSR, 1969.

报告一开始说到政治科学上贪腐的研究，其实是前几年突然火起来，就今年的
Midwest Political Science Association  的会议而言，就有8个跟贪腐有关的，
 其中一个是关于中国的 - Making Authoritarianism Work in China: Policy Activism,Anti-Corruption, and Representation。
2017年Midwest Political Science Association的会议关于贪腐研究的poster里，地区性研究当然有中国，另外还有韩国（近期在研究韩国，这下又多了点功课 ......）。比较有趣的是关于女性及贪腐的研究。 











现在全球也有好几个专门研究贪腐的中心，都是很新的：










• University of Sussex Centre for the Study of Corruption (2013 ?)




•The Transnational Research Institute on Corruption, Australian National University, (2010)

•Rutgers Institute on Anti-Corruption Studies (2018)

•European Research Centre for Anti-Corruption and State-Building, Hertie School of Governance (Berlin,2012）





连coursera 都有网络课程提供，Corruption.



下面是课程简介：


 You can't understand the world today if you don't understand corruption. Every day, we see high-level government officials resigning, governments changing, hundreds of thousands of people standing in squares, holding up signs, and often risking their lives to protest the corruption in the world. It's critically important to the health of our societies to understand what people are protesting against. We want to understand corruption. This course is designed to teach you about what corruption is, what causes corruption, the effects of corruption, and how to control corruption. We'll be talking about this from a global perspective using examples from all over the world. We'll also be talking about corruption from the perspective of countries, governments, businesses, citizens, and the people within these entities. At the end of this course, you'll have a better understanding of why the world is changing according to the path of corruption. If you're in a position to make changes within your respective entity, to make decisions about corruption, you'll be able to make better decisions.






从上面见到，“贪腐”不是那么简单，不是打击大老虎中老虎小老虎就行的(知道这个课程我又多点功课 ......)。

小习经常说“反腐反腐”，原来反腐是一门研究，是可以用研究结果去反的。我一直以为乌克兰比中国黑，但看到这里，我觉得中国人对反腐了解很少，最少乌克兰允许有这种研究，怎么说也比中国强点。









报告主题是苏共倒台后乌克兰的腐败问题，主题进入后，首先引用的是The Soviet Law Concept（下文ppt节录）。




• "Your law is the will of your class given the authority of a statute"

• Law is "the expression of the will of the classes which had won the victory and kept the governmental power in their hands." (V.I.Lenin)

"The Soviet law corresponds to the interests of the proletariat, organized into ruling class. The communist party is supposed to be the sole representative of these interests. The Soviet law must be presented in the party light and get Party decision." The courts and law-enforcement bodies have to carry out the policy pf the communist party as well as the Marxist-Lenin doctrine" (Vladimir Gsovski, p.3)








看看列宁那句话，中国宪法第一条关于人民民主专政完全符合，详情参阅稍后的《北京红色跨年游》报告，这句话我要引用在报告内的。之后那段就解释了为什么共产主义是独裁的，这点我以前也听专家说过，因为他们是代表无产阶级的，所以在一个无产阶级的社会，共产党是唯一一个有政权合法性的（科普；政权合法性regime legitimacy是一个政权得到普遍认同的原因）。






苏共倒台前后贪腐的区别是，倒台后官员利用法律去为个人利益服务，法律是一门工具，说到这里我想到之前内地有人“违法”卖vpn被罚款监禁的事- 《中国男子销售VPN代理服务获刑五年半》。




最后有谈到乌克兰的反腐政策，这又令我涨知识了，他们反腐政策很全面，针对不同官员的，采购的，法律等等，我放在文末给参考。其实以我仅有知识，也看到小习反腐的问题，打老虎是不能根治反腐的，一来这不能覆盖到全部贪腐的人，二来就是欠缺持续性，小习反腐，那万一下届领导不反呢？要持续下去，而且要覆盖到全部人，唯有政策上的改变，好像香港，就有廉政公署专门调查反腐，还有官员需要申报财产的。






最后，讲者介绍了自己的研究结果- assessment of the anti-corruption strategy implementation： successes and challenges。我没找到相关链接，但发现很多类似的研究，例如这个关于科索沃的。这篇是联合国对亚洲的研究。





看到这些深奥的反腐政策研究，我不禁问，小习那种算是什么？






附件：乌克兰的反腐政策（原文是6点的，我没抄到最后一点）


Sphere 1. Corruption prevention in public governmental bodies

1) Conduct anti-corruption expertise of election legislature

2) Restart financing of political parties at budget expense

3) Implement dear prevention policy of conflicts of interests

4) Establish transparent lobbying principles

5) Ensure civil control over decisions taken by elected officials

Sphere 2. Establishment of integrity public service

1) Amend the law On public service according to SIGMA standards

2) Establish system for declaring of public officials property, incomes and expenses

3) Set up control bodies over public servants ethic standards

4) Adopt the law on information provider protection

5) Conduct control over top-offidals integrity

Sphere 3. Anti-corruption policy in executive power bodies

1) Adopt anti-corruption programs for state and municipal enterprises

2) Constitute anti-corruption committees (departments) in executive power bodies
and private enterprises

3) Update approaches to anti-corruption policy

Sphere 4. Anti-corruption policy in public procurement sector

1) Reform the law On public procurement under international standards

2) Create Database of unfair participants of tender bid

3) Implement electronic procurement system

Sphere 5. Anti-corruption policy in judicial system

1) Diminish scope of judges immunities

2) Pursue policy to prevent conflicts of judges

3) Set up control over property, income, expenses declared by judges

4) Constitute body controlling integrity of judges

5) Hold anti-corruption trainings and test judges on professional qualification
